Weapon

Weapons in Genshin Impact are equipment that assists the player in fighting to deal more damage.
There are currently five different types of weapons in Genshin Impact, and each character is only able to wield one weapon type. Go to each weapon type's page (Bows, Catalysts, Claymores, Polearms, or Swords) for a list of all weapons of that type.
Weapons/Table also has a consolidated table for all weapons.
