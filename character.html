Character

Characters are obtainable units in Genshin Impact.
Characters can primarily be obtained by using Intertwined Fate or Acquaint Fate (purchased with Primogems though also available through Events, Quests or exchanged monthly for Masterless Stardust Masterless Stardust or Masterless Starglitter at Paimon's Bargains) to perform Wishes.
Noelle is a guaranteed character in the first 10 Wish in the Beginners' Wish.
